Best of

Perspective Sidewalk

For this project, we had to create a piece using perspective. Essentially, we had to repeat some visual element at smaller and smaller intervals to create a believable sense of depth. I created this process by adding a sidewalk with each part getting smaller as it gets farther away. You can also see this in the grass. The blades get smaller and less detailed the farther away.

Instruction Manual

For this project, we had to create an instruction manual for a piece of media. I picked The PowerPuff Girls because the intro is literally instructions. The manuals usually had a character explaining the instructions, so I just put the professor since he created the girls. I used vector in photoshop to create everything for this project.

Peacock Currency

For this piece, we had to imagine a world where animals replaced humans in a social situation, and we had to create currency for said animal. I picked peacocks, because I think if they were like humans, they would be egotistical and think they deserve to rule. I didn’t really have a goal with this one, but I’m really happy with how it turned out! I had to use a bunch of overlays and the hue and saturation adjustment, which was difficult to get correct.

Arts 102

Radial Dogs

For this project, we had to create radial symmetry, which is a type of symmetry where similar parts are arranged in a circular fashion around a central axis. I achieved this by arranging puppies in a circular fashion around a piece of bacon, like they're worshipping their favorite treat.

Symmetry

We had to create a piece that includes symmetry, which is when both sides of the canvas are exactly the same. I achieved this by creating identical girls with cat heads. I wanted them to have same vibes as the creepy twins from The Shining. They also have cats on their dresses, which I think is really cute.

Saturation

This was an assignment that we had to do on Photoshop to show our editing skills. We duplicated the same picture 6 times and changed the saturation for each of them to create this fun collage thing.

Arts 171

Propaganda Poster

We had to create a propaganda poster based on a piece of media. I decided to create a poster based on The Lorax, specifically the Onceler. Everthing, except the text, was created with vector in Photoshop.

SKillsUSA

This was my design for the SkillsUSA 2025 competition that we had to make for the class even if we weren't entering into the competition. I did enter my design into the competition and had to give a 5 minute presentation on how my design represents Michigan, my process in making it, and why mine should be picked. My design got second place!

Idiom

For this project, we had to create a color idiom in Photoshop and make it literal. An idiom is an expression that shouldn’t be taken literally, like how mine is “feeling blue” which usually means feeling sad. I did Blue Diamond from Steven Universe, who is a blue entity that can force her feelings onto others. I thought this was perfect, since Blue is blue and she’s lways sad. I had to use vector, hue and saturation adjustments, and shading for this project.

Personal Art

Emerald Gaze

This was a piece I did for my high school art class. We had to draw a part of the skeleton and use complimentary colors. Since I had already taken this class, I could just do whatever colors I wanted. I did use complimentary colors for the background and eyes though, along with the many other colors that go well with this art. I drew everything colored pencil.

Crimson Worry

This is also another piece I did entirely with colored pencil. This was one of those projects where I could do whatever I wanted. The emotion evolved over time and it looked like they were worried about something, so I added sweat drops. The bright red hair, yellow background, and blue drops are meant to make this piece based on primary colors.

Blue Somber

Blue Somber was one of the first mixed media pieces I did. It contains water color, acrylic paint, and colored pencil. I wanted to add "imperfections" to this piece because I think they're beautiful. For example, I added a bumpy nose, small lips, a tooth gap, downturned eyes, bushy eyebrows, and eye bags. I thought she looked a little sad about something, so that's where she got her name from.

Nightmare

This piece was for a high school assignment where we had to create a dream or nightmare. I created a nightmare that I had experienced where I was sinking underwater and saw a huge lady with long, flowing hair. I created this in Procreate.

Emblem

This was also done in Procreate. We had to create a logo for ourselves that include things that symbolize us. I added the sun and moon because my dad and I love to look at up at the stars. I added a music note in the middle, because I love music and it's such a huge part of my life. Finally, I added a lot of blue, because blue has always been my favorite color.

Anatomy

This was a piece I did to practice anatomy, mainly back muscles. I like how it turned out. I originally made it in grayscale and then I added gradient maps. I adjusted these colors until I liked the result.
